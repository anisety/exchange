Welcome. This site documents the exchange **REST** and **WebSocket** APIs you will use during the competition. You can bootstrap and recover over **REST**, stream aggregated market data and private events over **WebSocket**, and keep your client in sync with a single public **data stream** channel.

## Base URLs

- **REST:** `https://exchange.jamesxu.dev`
- **WebSocket:** `wss://exchange.jamesxu.dev/ws`

## What to build

1. Confirm who you are with [Get User](/rest/user).
2. Load [Get Markets](/rest/markets) and cache `status`, `tick_size`, `min_order_quantity`, and any configured price bounds for each symbol you trade.
3. Bootstrap risk and positions with [Get Portfolio](/rest/portfolio).
4. Subscribe to the public **`data`** stream for aggregated depth updates.
5. Handle **`fill`**, **`order_state`**, **`admin_message`**, and **`resync_required`** on WebSocket.
6. After reconnects, halts, or settlement, refresh state from REST so your client matches the server.

## Rules at a glance

- **Prices and quantities** are integers.
- **`side`** is **`BUY`** or **`SELL`**.
- **Standard teams** have a worst-case **±1000** net position cap per market (unless organizers assign a different role).
- The public WebSocket **`data`** stream is aggregated by price level and batched roughly every **100ms**.
- **Authenticated** REST and WebSocket trading share one bucket: **500 operations per 10 seconds** by default. Details: [Rate limits](/reference/rate-limits).
- **After settlement**, positions for that market flatten at the settlement price and PnL is realized there.
