## Before trading

1. Collect your **API key** from the organizers.
2. Call [Get User](/rest/user) and verify **`team_number`** and **`trader_id`**.
3. From [Get Markets](/rest/markets), cache each **`market_id`**, **`status`**, **`tick_size`**, **`min_order_quantity`**, optional **`min_price`** / **`max_price`**, and **`reference_price`** you rely on.
4. Call [Get Portfolio](/rest/portfolio) so you know positions and how limits apply.
5. Open a **WebSocket** if you want live books, fills, or low-latency order messages.

## While trading

### Order and risk rules

- **`price`**, **`quantity`**, and **`remaining`** are integers.
- **`side`** is **`BUY`** or **`SELL`**.
- Default accounts are subject to a **┬▒1000** worst-case net position cap **per market** (unless organizers assign a different role).
- **`price`** must align to the market **`tick_size`**.
- If a market publishes **`min_price`** or **`max_price`**, your limit price must stay within that inclusive band.
- **`quantity`** must be at least **`min_order_quantity`**.
- Authenticated REST and authenticated WebSocket trading share **500 operations / 10 seconds** by default. See [Rate limits](/reference/rate-limits) for the exact list.

### Suggested architecture

**REST** ŌĆö bootstrap and recovery:

- [Get User](/rest/user)
- [Get Portfolio](/rest/portfolio)
- [Get Balance](/rest/balance) (optional: per-asset free vs locked)
- [Get Open Orders](/rest/open-orders)
- [Get Fills](/rest/fills)
- [Get Markets](/rest/markets)

**WebSocket** ŌĆö live behavior:

- Public aggregated **`data`** stream (batched every **100ms**)
- Your **`fill`** and **`order_state`** events
- **`submit_order`**, **`cancel_order`**, **`amend_order`** when you want socket-based entry

**Note:** only **one** market-data subscription per connection todayŌĆöuse extra sockets for more symbols.

### When the exchange accepts an order

- The order may **fill**, **rest**, or **partially fill then rest**.
- The REST response may include immediate **`fills`**.
- **`resting: true`** means remaining size is on the book.
- While connected, **`order_state`** and **`fill`** carry your live account-side updates.
- For **your** exact resting lines, combine **`order_state`** with REST [open orders](/rest/open-orders).
- A single **`delta`** can bundle multiple eventsŌĆöalways check **`start_sequence`** and advance to **`sequence`**.

## Recovery during the event

If you receive **`resync_required`**:

1. Drop local book state for the affected market.
2. **Subscribe again** and rebuild from the new snapshot.
3. If the message concerns **user** events, refresh account data over **REST**.

Full detail: [Recovery and resync](/reference/recovery-and-resync).

## After settlement

When a market is **settled**:

- Trading stops for that symbol.
- Open orders for that market are cleared.
- Positions flatten at the **`settlement_price`** organizers set.
- Unrealized PnL for that market becomes **realized**.

Refresh right away: [Get Markets](/rest/markets), [Get Portfolio](/rest/portfolio), [Get Balance](/rest/balance), [Get Positions](/rest/positions), [Get Open Orders](/rest/open-orders), [Get Fills](/rest/fills).

Organizer rankings are private. Competitor clients should treat settlement as the moment PnL for that market stabilizes on the server.
